David Bau - Bio and Headshot

David Bau is Assistant Professor at Northeastern University and Director of the National Deep Inference Fabric, researching the emergent internal mechanisms of deep generative networks with applications in Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision. Author of "Dissection of Deep Networks" (MIT PhD) and creator of ROME, Network Dissection, and GAN Paint. Published in PNAS, Neurips, ICLR, CVPR, SIGGRAPH, ECCV, EMNLP, ICCV. Formerly at Google, contributing to Google Image Search and Google Talk. Co-author of the widely used textbook Numerical Linear Algebra.
